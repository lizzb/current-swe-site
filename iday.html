This year's SWE Industry Day will take place on Thursday, October 17, 2013. SWE Industry Day is Northwestern's largest engineering and technology oriented career fair.
For more information, please contact Program Director Alexandra Lamens at swe.IndustryDay.2013@gmail.com

For Students

Industry Day will be held in Norris University Center from 5 - 9 PM. Doors will close at 8:30.

SWE Industry Day is open to all Northwestern students and alumni. Students do not need to pre-register, but a Wildcard is required for entry. Please wear business attire.
